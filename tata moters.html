About us

Agile, new-age and future-ready

1 million+
Vehicles sold annually

Part of the USD 128 billion Tata Group, Tata Motors, a USD 42 billion
organisation, is a leading global automobile manufacturer of cars, utility
vehicles, pick-ups, trucks and buses. We are India’s market leader in
commercial vehicles and amongst the top three in the passenger vehicles
market. We prioritise human centricity with technological prowess and
engineering excellence to make cargo and passenger mobility safer,
smarter and greener.
